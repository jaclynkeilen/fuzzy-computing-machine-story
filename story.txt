This is Jaclyn's story! 

Today was the day of the biggest game of the UW football season: the Minnesota game. Johnathan and his friends were
really wishing for a Wisconsin victory this year, after Minnesota won last year and ended the longest win streak in the
rivalry. They stayed up all night, unable to sleep.

As the game started, everyone could feel the tension building up in the air. The crowd was utterly silent the whole time. The ball was in Jonathan Taylor's hand. He dashed pass all the defenders, jumping over them, leaving a trail of dust behing. He was approaching the touchdown area when suddenly one player from the opposing team tripped him down to the ground! Everyone in the audience were shocked...
